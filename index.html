History

Background:

    On 23 March 2019, Lize Helesta debuted together with Ange Katrina and Inui Toko as part of the Sanbaka. On the same day, she reached 10,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

2019:

    On 29 April, Lize reached 50,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

    On 8 July, Lize reached 100,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

    On 20 August, she unveiled her summer outfit model.

    On 23 September, Lize reached 150,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

    On 18 October, she and the rest of Sanbaka received updated models.

    On 4 December, she unveiled her dress outfit in commemoration of her reaching 50,000 subscribers.

    On 8 December, Lize reached 200,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

2020:

    On 22 January, Lize unveiled her official 3D model via YouTube livestream.

    On 25 February, Lize reached 250,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

    On 5 April, Lize begins a regular radio show with Suzuhara Lulu, にじさんじ presents リゼるる Listen, on national broadcast produced by A&G every Sunday on 8 PM JST.

    On 10 May, Lize reached 300,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

    On 19 May Lize begins a regular Niconico Namahousou with seiyuu Hana Tamegai, who is known for voicing Risa Matoba from Idolm@ster Cinderella Girls, titled 集貝はなとリゼ・ヘルエスタの『皇女にタメでもいいですか！？』 or shortened ためリゼ. The first stream aired on 22 May 2020.

    On 25 May Lize released her solo image song, ハッピーエンドをはじめから (Happy Endo wo Hajime Kara--Beginning A Happy End), composed by TOKOTOKO (NishizawasanP).

    On 2 August, Lize reached 350,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

    On 4 November, Lize reached 400,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

2021:

    On 1 February, Lize reached 450,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

    On 16 April, Lize reached 500,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel, making her the 7th NIJISANJI Liver to reach this milestone.

    On 7 August, Lize reached 550,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

    On 9 December, Lize reached 600,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

2022:

    On 17 May, Lize reached 650,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.

    On 29 September, Lize reached 700,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel.
